CHRIS RYAN
Chris Ryan isn't complex but he's a good guy at heart. But also, he's a fuckboi, & Sandy is wasting her time trying to make him love her. He's in a cool band, that's not very good. Sandy is obsessed with him, & Kimber has a secret crush on him too. He's really hot & nice if you really get to know him. He'll fight the bad guys to protect the people he loves.
BRENT

He's the star quarterback, he's a hunk, his dad owns the town's McDonald's, and he's got narcissistic personality disorder. In other words, the world is his oyster! He always gets what he wants, and what he wants right now is to have sex with Sandy. Also he wants to piss off Kimber. Will this douchebag win? Hell no. Because this is a made up story.

DK

DK is a great guy. He's cool and confident and eccentric. He's funny, smart and the second string quarterback. He respects himself, knows his worth, and so he is able to respect and appreciate women too. He has an unrequited crush on Sandy because he thinks that she's smart, cool and he likes that she's a top-notch musician.

McCarty

McCarty is the marching band director and Sandy's first friend at her new school. He represents the loving and present father figure that Sandy has been missing. He tries to help Sandy and to give her good advice. But, like most teenagers, Sandy ignores him and goes on a self-destructive rampage instead.

Dooney

Dooney is Brent's lackey goon. He's big and dumb and thinks Brent is the coolest and best guy in the whole world. He's a linebacker on the football team. He's not a bad guy, just the subservient goon of a bad guy.
