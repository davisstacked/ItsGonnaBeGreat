Oh SANDY!

An overachieving band-geek virgin with a heart of gold

When Sandy's parents get a divorce, Sandy is shoved out of her intellectual comfort zone and into a new life in small town Ohio.

Sandy's father was the one who made the rules, so now that she's just with her mom, she's sneaking out of her house, going to parties and getting away with it.

Instead of dealing with her feelings of her father's perceived abandonment, Sandy projects her intense feelings onto her new boyfriend Chris Ryan.

Sandy is oh so hopelessly devoted to Chris Ryan! She'd do anything to get him to love her, including becoming a Bad Girl and making a deal with school alpha slut Kimber.

She wants Kimber to like her. More than Chris Ryan even, there is KIMBER. Through her friendship with Kimber, she's becoming a young woman and exploring her sexuality for the first time.

Society had always told Sandy that being a slut was a bad thing (other than when they were using female sexuality to sell her things like clothes and makeup), but here's Kimber being a slut AND the most popular girl in school AND Chris Ryan has a crush on her?! So why does everyone love Kimber? Sandy is desperate to find out.
