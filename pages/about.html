Leah Davis is a software engineer and yoga instructor based in Los Angeles, California.

She wrote It's Gonna Be Great! after having a sleepover with her 12-year-old cousin. Leah was exhausted from a recent breakup, while her cousin, having never dated, was So Excited about boys and was sure that Dating Was Gonna Be Great!

Leah thought this was so cute and tragic that she wrote a whole movie about it.

This movie is a road-map for young women and men everywhere on how to navigate the opposite-sex for the first time - the good, the bad, and the really bad.
